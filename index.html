Portfolio Campaign 7: "Bliss" (Series) book cover designs 

This campagin comprises of 3 book covers, each from my personal sci-fi fantasy hybrid- graphic novel series: Bliss. Despite this project being a personal one, it presented plenty of challenges in its own way, mainly stemming from the fact that I had (and still have) alot of vested energy and passion for this particular project. 

Each of the covers started as hand drawn sketches that I then scanned and turned into vector PNGs which were manipulated in PhotoShop to give their final appearance. I found a license-free leather texture that I also manipulated in Photoshop to give the appearance of being stained the various colors.
